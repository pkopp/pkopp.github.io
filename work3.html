FANDANGO: LIVE!

ROLE: Research, Project management, User interviews, Usability Testing, Wireframing, & Prototyping

SUMMARY

Fandango is the premier provider of movie related media. The primary function of the app is to sell tickets to movie goers. This concept piece fleshes out the idea of expanding Fandango's market to include ticket sales to concerts and other live events. The hope is to capitalize on Fandango's exisiting customer base with an added service.

THE OPPORTUNITY

Our 4 person design team was tasked with creating an app that would broaden Fandango's market to include ticket sales to live events. creating an application that would capitalize on Fandago's exisiting ticketing platform, customer base, and branding.
